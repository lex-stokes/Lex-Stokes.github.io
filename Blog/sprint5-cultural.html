What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

Self awareness ensures that people are aware of the actions of others around them, this can stimulate growth as it encourages you to talk to the people around you and see if you can gain knowledge or pass it on. Empathy’s role is to allow us to work in an environment where everyone is taken care of in the way they need, it stops us from directing rage or frustration at others and notice if the team has any issues and how we should tackle them. And values have a role everywhere, they are the driving force behind why we make an action. A set of good values will help a team thrive together and create the best product that the team can make.

They were so good for my mental health and I wish more educational facilities would provide this.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Reflecting on myself! And writing it all in a form that makes sense. Then posting it for others to read. Ekk!!

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Because how we communicate within a workplace defines how we work, a system of good, clear communication with the team that we are working with means that we will progress through work at a timely pace with all involved knowing what their goals and time frames are. It creates a healthier workplace that encourages team members to contribute and feel comfortable giving feedback.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer.

Not at all, while one can learn coding at their own pace with a lot of self directed study, it is quite hard to navigate the core skill set through google search and having links to videos and raising awareness of the different things our brains do while we learn and interact means that we have a better understanding of what we are going through as we learn a new skill set. This allows our brain to not stress so much as we put it through its paces to try and learn. Giving us hope makes a difference.
